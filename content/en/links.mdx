## Related Documentation and Communities

Here are Nix-related documentation and communities for learning Nix and NixOS. Newcomers should start with the [NixOS & Flakes Book](https://nixos-and-flakes.thiscute.world/) by [Ryan Yin](https://github.com/ryan4yin), a highly-praised community tutorial. The NixOS Chinese community also offers intuitive [concept illustrations](https://nixos-cn.org/tutorials/concept/) for beginners.

- [Official Nix Documentation](https://nix.dev/)
- [Official Nixpkgs Documentation](https://nixos.org/manual/nixpkgs/stable/)
- [Official NixOS Documentation](https://nixos.org/manual/nixos/stable/index.html)
- [NixOS Chinese Community](https://nixos-cn.org/)

Here are Loongson-related information sites and communities. Join these if you're interested in Loongson or have questions:

- [Are We Loong Yet?](https://areweloongyet.com/)
- [Loongson Enthusiast Community](https://www.loongbbs.cn/)
- [Loongson Open Source Community](https://github.com/loongson-community)

Planning to buy Loongson devices? Check the [LoongArch Compatibility Database](https://loong123.cn/) for compatible hardware or read the [LoongArch Device Purchasing Guide for Developers](https://darkyzhou.net/articles/buying-a-loongarch-device).
